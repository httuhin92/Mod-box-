About MOD BOX

MOD BOX is an app store where users can upload various types of apps and other users can download those apps. It is a platform where developers can share their apps and users can use them.

App Upload Rules

  1. First login to your account
  2. Go to upload page
  3. Fill the form with correct information
  4. Select app category
  5. Provide app name, icon, download link etc.
  6. Add screenshots and description
  7. Click upload button
  8. Your app will be sent for admin approval

App Edit and Delete Rules

  1. Login to your account
  2. Go to app list page
  3. Find the app you want to edit or delete
  4. Click edit or delete button next to the app
  5. Make necessary changes or confirm you want to delete the app
  6. Save or confirm

Money Counter Rules

  • You will get $0.50 for each app upload
  • You will get $0.01 for each download of your uploaded app
  • You will get $0.02 for each review
  • You can withdraw when minimum $500
  • Click withdraw button in balance page to withdraw

Profile Edit Rules

  1. Login to your account
  2. Go to settings page
  3. Click edit profile button
  4. Change your profile picture link and name
  5. Enter new password if you want to change password
  6. Click save button
